Reina's Mochi Icecream Recipe

For the Filling: 1 pint (approx. 2 cups) strawberry ice cream.(br) For the Mochi Dough:(br) 1 cup sweet glutinous rice flour (e.g., Mochiko brand).(br) ¼ cup granulated sugar.(br) ¾ to 1 cup liquid (either water, milk, or a mix of both).(br) Optional: 1–2 drops pink food coloring or 1 tbsp strawberry puree for flavor/color.(br) For Dusting: ½ cup cornstarch or potato starch (to prevent sticking).
